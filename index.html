Director's Message

In this race of urbanization, the children of those living in rural areas are not able to get proper environment in today’s competitive era which is felt only through primary, middle education. Generally, a level of teachers or institutions avoid going to rural areas. But there is still a long line of talented students in village whose dream is to go into medical and engineering. For which it is not possible for everyone to go to the city and in the absence of proper guidance, the students go astray.
In order to bridge this situation, I have established my Parasmani Classes for class 6th to 12th in this rural area which is the land of Baba Draveshwarnath.
My effort is to make the students who come to us with a dream come true. That is why I made the Panchaline of my institute.
“Dream yours, we will do it”
